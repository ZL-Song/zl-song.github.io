Research

I currently work as a postdoc. researcher @ Westlake University w/ Prof. Jing Huang. I previously did my Ph.D. in Theoretical and Computational Chemistry, mentored by Prof. Peng Tao @ SMU. I do research in computational chemistry & biophysics, focused on: (1) Chain-of-States Methods for Transition Path Theory; (2) Computational Structural Biology; and (3) Deep Learning for Biochemical Discovery. My research has been supported (as independent PI) by the NSFC, NSFZJ, and CPSF.
