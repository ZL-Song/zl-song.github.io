Research

I work currently as a postdoc. researcher @ Westlake University w/ Prof. Jing Huang. I did my Ph.D. in Theoretical and Computational Chemistry w/ Prof. Peng Tao @ SMU. My research focuses on computational chemistry and biophysics, eps.: (1) Chain-of-States Methods for Transition Path Theory; (2) Computational Structural Biology; and (3) Deep Learning for Biochemical Discovery.
I receive research grants (as independent PI) from the NSFC, the NSFZJ, and the CPSF.
